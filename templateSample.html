Executive Summary:
Our social platform now serves approximately 500 verified users across member cohorts and ministry partners. Daily active users averaged 36% this quarter, up 9% from the prior period, driven by steadier posting cadence and clearer onboarding prompts. Median session length rose to 6m 40s, and the content save rate improved to 22%, indicating stronger intent to revisit posts. Creator activity broadened: 27% of users published at least one item per week and 11% hosted live discussions, with first-time creators accounting for a third of all new content. Support response time continued to decline, averaging 3h 12m on weekdays and 7h 05m on weekends. Infrastructure remained stable with >99.95% uptime following the CDN cache tuning; media delivery errors fell by 41%. Moderation backlog is near zero after refined flag queues and lightweight appeal workflows. Looking ahead, we will: (1) expand prompts that surface evergreen content to new members, (2) pilot topic subscriptions with digest controls, and (3) tighten analytics dimensions for cohort comparisons. These steps focus on retention, creator enablement, and observability. All figures are preliminary pending finance reconciliation of paid services and storage credits. A finalized report with audited financial metrics will follow the accounting close.
